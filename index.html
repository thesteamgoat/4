Welcome!

Light energy is a form of energy that can be seen and travels in waves.  It is made when particles with an electric charge vibrate.  Light is the only form of energy that can travel through the vacuum of empty space.  Energy can neither be created nor destroyed, only transformed.  And light energy can be made in multiple ways such as when electrical energy changes to light in a lamp or when the chemical energy stored in wood burns.  Light travels in a straight line out in all directions from its source.  Explore this website to learn about the various ways light interacts with matter.
