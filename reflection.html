Reflection describes how light bounces off of smooth, shiny surfaces such as mirrors and placid bodies of water.  Light rays bounce off at the same angle they enter the surface.  Think about what happens when you bounce a ball off a wall.  If you throw it straight at the wall, it will bounce straight back to you.  If you bounce it at an angle, it will bounce away from you at the same angle.  Mirrors, sunglasses, tinted windows, spoons, and placid bodies of water all reflect light.
